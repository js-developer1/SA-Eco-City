Our Plan For The UN Smart Devlopment Goals



What are the UN Sustainability Devlopment Goals?

The UN Sustainability Devlopment Goals are a 17 goals that are aimed to acheive sustainability by 2050.

What Goals Are We Working Towards And How Are We Acheiving Them?

  • We can acheive sustainbility goals #1 and #8 by providing enough jobs for everyone to get a good high paying job which elimantes poverty and allows the economy to grow.

  • We can acheive sustainbility goal #3 by providing high quality, and accessible healthcare to everyone in the city.

  • We can also acheive sustainbility goal #4 by providing high quality, and accessible schools to everyone in the city.

  • We can acheive sustainbility goal #6 by having sewage plants which clean the sewage water effectivly and also with the least amouont of pollution. We can also acheive sustainbility goal #6 by pumping water from fresh sources.

  • We can acheive sustainbility goal #7 by implementing solar panel and carbon capture technology to generate clean electricity.

  • We can acheive sustainbility goal #12 by having policies that force the citizen to reuse, reduce, and recycle, as much as they can.

  • We can acheive sustainbility goal #13 by having lot's of greenery and also implementing carbon capture technology. This technology will capture emitted CO2 and convert it to energy hence combating the climate change threat.

  • We are acheiving both goal #11 and goal #17 by building this city.
