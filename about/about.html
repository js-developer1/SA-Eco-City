About SA Eco City

Location Of SA Eco City

SA Eco City is located in South-Western South Africa in between the towns of Rooidriffie, Oudekraal, Remhoogte, and Riviersonderend. SA Eco City is seperated into 2 parts by the R326 highway. SA Eco City is 155km away from Cape Town, one of South Africa's capitals.
