About SA Eco City

Location Of SA Eco City

SA Eco City is located in South-Western South Africa in between the towns of Rooidriffie, Oudekraal, Remhoogte, and Riviersonderend. SA Eco City is seperated into 2 parts by the R326 highway. SA Eco City is 155km away from Cape Town, one of South Africa's capitals.

Latitude: -34.288605°N

Longitude: 19.763770°W

Geography Of SA Eco City

The reason we chose to build SA Eco City here is because of the geography of the land. The land is relativly flat, with no physical features that can disrupt the construction of the city. Another reason we chose to build here is because the climate is perfect for farming with warm tempratures and a good amount of rain days to water the soil for free. The citizens of SA Eco City will also enjoy the climate of the area where SA Eco City is being built.
